Welcome cadet, you have been safely transported to the outpost. Now your flight lesson will begin. Going through the yellow portal ends the tutorial. Press "Enter" to progress the dialogue.
Move your mouse horizontally to pitch your ship, move your mouse vertically to roll your ship. 
Press Q and E to move your ship on the yaw axis. 
Use the mouse scroll wheel to change between weapons (cannons, missiles and lazers).
Press left mouse button to shoot, and right mouse button to lock on target when the retical is on an enemy.
Missiles require a locked target, and to lock on a target, you need to have missiles chosen.
Press Shift to accelerate and CTRL to use the brakes or move backwards. 
The blue circle map on the bottom and middle of the screen is a radar, enemies and points of interest will be shown on there.
The first bar from the left is a thrust bar, it will be yellow when you accelerate and yellow/orange when use the brakes or move backwards. 
The second bar from the left is a cargo bar, it is gray/blue, it shows you your cargo capacity and how much cargo you have picked up.
The third bar from the left is green, it shows you the hulls health.
The last bar from the left is blue, it shows you the health of your sheild. Press ESC to pause. 
Good job cadet! Now you are ready to begin your first mission.
Press "M" to open the mission select menu, and every mission has a small text to quickly brief you on the mission.
When you have picked a mission, you can then track it on your radar.
Good luck cadet!
To end the tutorial, go through the yellow portal.
